### Q: "How do you determine the winner?"

**A**: "We use a weighted scoring system: Equity (30%), Efficiency (25%), Overload Avoidance (20%), Travel Safety (15%), Feasibility (10%). The weights reflect disaster management priorities - fairness matters most, but speed also counts."

### Q: "What if two playbooks tie?"

**A**: "If overall scores are within 5 points, we call it a tie. Then commanders can choose based on their specific priorities - maybe they prefer higher efficiency over higher equity for this particular disaster."

**A**: "We cap at 4 for performance and UI clarity. But the algorithm supports unlimited playbooks - we just need to optimize the display. For most operations, comparing 3-4 strategies is sufficient."

**A**: "About 1-2 seconds for 4 full playbooks. Each playbook runs a complete simulation: mission generation, communications planning, resource allocation, and scoring. It's fast enough for interactive exploration."

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### "Select All" button not working

- Make sure you have at least 2 playbooks in library
- Check browser console for errors

### Battle results empty

- Verify playbooks are selected (cyan highlight)
- Check that "Run Battle Mode" was clicked
- Look for console error messages

### Scores all show 100

- This might be correct if playbooks are well-designed
- Check Failure Points panel - should list any issues
- Try creating intentionally flawed playbooks to test

### Winner promotion not working

- Check that a battle has been run
- Verify winner is identified (green row)
- Check console for errors
